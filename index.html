The Lovato GL Isolator is a robust, industrial-grade switch disconnector designed to safely isolate power in heavy-duty electrical systems.

What it does

It ensures safe disconnection of circuits, maintenance protection, and reliable power cutoff in compliance with international standards.

Why it matters

With its durable build, compact design, and reliable performance, the Lovato GL Isolator reduces downtime and enhances operational safety across industrial sites.
